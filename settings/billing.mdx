This guide covers everything about managing your Trilo subscription, billing, and credits system.

## Accessing Billing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Billing">
    Navigate to the **Billing** section.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Only workspace owners can access billing settings.
</Info>

## Subscription Plans

### Available Plans

Trilo offers different plans to fit your team's needs:

| Plan | Best For | Key Features |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Free** | Small teams trying Trilo | Core features, limited members |
| **Pro** | Growing teams | More members, advanced features |
| **Team** | Larger organizations | Full features, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Large enterprises | Custom limits, dedicated support |

### Plan Comparison

| Feature | Free | Pro | Team | Enterprise |
|---------|------|-----|------|------------|
| Members | Limited | More | Unlimited | Custom |
| Storage | Basic | More | Large | Custom |
| AI credits | Limited | More | Abundant | Custom |
| Support | Community | Email | Priority | Dedicated |
| Features | Core | Advanced | Full | Custom |

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Current Plan

In Billing settings, you'll see:

- Your current plan name
- Billing cycle (monthly/yearly)
- Next billing date
- Current usage vs. limits

### Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade">
    Find the upgrade button in Billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose new plan">
    Select the plan you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select billing cycle">
    Monthly or yearly (yearly often has discounts).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payment info">
    Add or confirm payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Complete the upgrade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upgrades take effect immediately.

### Downgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Change Plan">
    Find plan options in Billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select lower plan">
    Choose the plan to downgrade to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review impacts">
    See what features you'll lose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Downgrade takes effect at end of billing period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Downgrading may result in losing access to features and hitting limits. Review impacts before confirming.
</Warning>

### Canceling Subscription

To cancel your paid subscription:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Cancel subscription**
3. Provide feedback (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

After canceling:
- Access continues until the end of the billing period
- Workspace reverts to Free plan
- Data is preserved (within Free plan limits)

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Payment Method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Payment Methods">
    Find this in Billing settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add">
    Add a new payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details">
    Add credit card or other payment info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The method is available for billing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- Other methods may vary by region

### Updating Payment Method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Payment Methods">
    In Billing settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit or add new">
    Update existing or add new method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set as default">
    Choose which method to use for billing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Removing Payment Method

1. Find the payment method
2. Click **Remove**
3. Confirm removal

<Info>
  You can't remove the last payment method while on a paid plan.
</Info>

## Credits System

### What Are Credits?

Credits power AI features in Trilo:

- AI coworker conversations
- Image generation
- Advanced AI capabilities

### Viewing Credit Balance

In Billing, see:
- Current credit balance
- Usage this month
- Included credits with your plan
- Additional credits purchased

### Purchasing Credits

If you need more credits:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Credits">
    Find Credits section in Billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose amount">
    Select credit package to purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete purchase">
    Credits are added immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Auto Top-Up

Automatically purchase credits when balance is low:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Auto Top-Up">
    Toggle on in Credits settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set threshold">
    When to trigger purchase (e.g., below 100 credits).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set amount">
    How many credits to purchase each time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invoices and Receipts

### Viewing Invoices

Find past invoices:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Invoices**
2. See list of all invoices
3. Click any invoice to view/download

### Invoice Contents

Each invoice includes:
- Date and invoice number
- Plan charges
- Credit purchases
- Tax information
- Payment method used

### Downloading Receipts

- Click **Download** on any invoice
- PDF format for your records
- Contains all billing details

## Billing Information

### Company Details

For invoices, provide:
- Company name
- Billing address
- Tax ID (if applicable)

### Updating Billing Info

1. Go to Billing Information
2. Edit company details
3. Save changes
4. Future invoices use new info

## Usage & Limits

### Viewing Usage

See your current consumption:

| Metric | Where to Find |
|--------|---------------|
| Members | Settings > Members |
| Storage | Settings > Billing > Usage |
| Credits | Settings > Billing > Credits |

### Approaching Limits

When nearing limits:
- You'll receive notifications
- Dashboard shows warnings
- Consider upgrading before hitting limits

### Exceeding Limits

What happens when you exceed limits:
- Some features may be limited
- Upgrade prompts appear
- Grace periods may apply

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment failed">
    - Check payment method is valid
    - Ensure sufficient funds
    - Try a different payment method
    - Contact your bank if issues persist
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't access billing">
    - Only workspace owners can access billing
    - Contact the workspace owner
    - Check you're in the correct workspace
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invoice missing">
    - Check spam folder for email receipts
    - View all invoices in Billing settings
    - Contact support if invoice is needed
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wrong charge">
    - Review invoice details
    - Check for usage-based charges
    - Contact support for billing questions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
