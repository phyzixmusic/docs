## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Desktop app won't open">
    Try these steps:
    1. Restart your computer
    2. Re-download the latest version from our website
    3. Check that your OS meets the minimum requirements
    4. On Mac, try right-clicking and selecting "Open" if you see a security warning
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Not receiving notifications">
    Make sure notifications are enabled:
    - **Desktop**: Check your system notification settings
    - **Mobile**: Go to Settings > Apps > Trilo > Notifications
    - **In-app**: Check Settings > Notifications in Trilo
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mobile app is slow">
    Try these solutions:
    1. Close and reopen the app
    2. Check your internet connection
    3. Update to the latest version
    4. Clear the app cache in your device settings
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
